About Burgenton

Building Reliable Equipment Solutions for Africa’s Growth

Burgenton Equipment was established with a clear purpose: to provide reliable, high-performance equipment that supports Africa’s construction, industrial, and agricultural development.

2-Year Warranty Genuine Spare Parts Technical Support Dealer Network Fast Delivery
Our Story

Bridging Africa’s Equipment Gap

Africa is experiencing rapid growth in infrastructure, urban development, and agricultural industrialization. Yet many businesses continue to face challenges in accessing equipment that combines performance, reliability, technical support, and long-term value. Burgenton Equipment was created to bridge this gap.

Our goal is to provide equipment solutions that empower contractors, developers, processors, manufacturers, and entrepreneurs to operate more efficiently and compete more effectively. Today, Burgenton Equipment serves customers across multiple sectors, supplying equipment designed to support the continent’s development ambitions while delivering exceptional value to our customers.

As infrastructure expands and industries grow across the continent, businesses require equipment they can depend on to deliver results efficiently and profitably — and we believe that reliable equipment is more than a machine, it is an investment in productivity, growth, and opportunity.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted equipment brand

Supporting sustainable growth through innovative, reliable, and accessible equipment solutions.

Our Mission

High-performance equipment, backed by dependable support

To provide high-performance construction and agro-processing equipment backed by dependable support, genuine spare parts, and customer-focused service that enables businesses to build, produce, and grow with confidence.

Infrastructure is expanding, cities are growing, industries are developing, and agricultural transformation is creating new opportunities for economic advancement. Our mission extends beyond supplying machines — we are committed to supporting the projects, businesses, and industries helping shape Africa’s future.
