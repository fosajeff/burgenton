Concrete Pumps

Efficient concrete placement for faster project delivery.

2-Year Warranty Genuine Spare Parts Technical Support Dealer Network Fast Delivery

Burgenton Concrete Pumps are designed to deliver concrete quickly, accurately, and efficiently to locations that are difficult or time-consuming to reach using traditional methods.

Whether pumping concrete to upper floors, across large construction sites, or into confined areas, our concrete pumps help contractors improve productivity, reduce labour requirements, and maintain consistent concrete placement.

Built for reliability and performance, Burgenton Concrete Pumps support residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects where speed, precision, and efficiency are essential.

For maximum productivity, Burgenton Concrete Pumps integrate seamlessly with our concrete production equipment. Burgenton Concrete Pumps are built to support contractors working on everything from residential developments and commercial buildings to major infrastructure projects, delivering reliable performance wherever concrete needs to be placed.

Why Choose Burgenton Concrete Pumps?

Every Burgenton Concrete Pump is engineered to provide dependable performance, efficient concrete delivery, and long-term reliability. Supported by genuine spare parts, technical expertise, operator guidance, and comprehensive warranty protection, our pumps help contractors complete projects faster while maintaining high construction standards.
