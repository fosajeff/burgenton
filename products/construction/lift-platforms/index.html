Burgenton Lift Platforms provide safe, reliable, and efficient elevated working solutions for construction, industrial maintenance, warehousing, facility management, and infrastructure projects.

Designed to improve productivity while enhancing workplace safety, our lift platforms enable operators to work confidently at height across a wide range of indoor and outdoor applications.

Whether carrying out building maintenance, equipment installation, warehouse operations, or construction activities, Burgenton Lift Platforms help businesses work smarter, safer, and more efficiently.

Why Choose Burgenton

Why Choose Burgenton Lift Platforms?

Every Burgenton Lift Platform is engineered to provide dependable performance, operator safety, and long-term reliability. Supported by genuine spare parts, technical assistance, operator training, and warranty protection, our equipment helps customers complete elevated work efficiently while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Available Configurations

  • Self-Propelled Hydraulic Scissor Lifts
    Stable vertical lifting on level surfaces — ideal for warehouses, factories, commercial buildings, and indoor construction work.
  • Crawler (Track) Scissor Lifts
    Crawler tracks for enhanced stability and mobility on uneven or challenging terrain — suited to outdoor sites with soft or rough ground.
  • Articulating Boom Lifts
    Exceptional horizontal and vertical reach, allowing operators to work around obstacles and access difficult-to-reach areas safely.
