Mobile / Compact Concrete Batching Plants

Efficient concrete production wherever your project takes you.

2-Year Warranty Genuine Spare Parts Technical Support Dealer Network Fast Delivery

Burgenton Mobile and Compact Concrete Batching Plants provide a flexible, reliable, and efficient solution for producing high-quality concrete directly at or near your project site.

Designed for contractors, developers, and infrastructure companies, these plants combine accurate batching, ease of transportation, and quick installation, making them ideal for projects where mobility, space efficiency, and consistent concrete quality are essential.

Whether supporting housing developments, road construction, commercial buildings, or remote infrastructure projects, Burgenton batching plants help improve productivity while reducing transportation costs and project delays.
