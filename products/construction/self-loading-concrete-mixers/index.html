Self-Loading Concrete Mixers

Produce, mix, transport, and discharge concrete using a single machine.

2-Year Warranty Genuine Spare Parts Technical Support Dealer Network Fast Delivery

Burgenton Self-Loading Mobile Concrete Mixers are engineered to simplify concrete production by combining material loading, batching, mixing, transportation, and concrete discharge into one highly efficient machine.

Designed for contractors, developers, infrastructure companies, and concrete producers, these versatile machines eliminate the need for multiple pieces of equipment, helping businesses reduce operating costs, improve productivity, and maintain complete control over concrete production on-site.

Whether working on housing developments, commercial buildings, road projects, industrial facilities, or remote construction sites, Burgenton Self-Loading Mobile Concrete Mixers deliver reliable performance wherever your project takes you.

Construction projects across Africa often require equipment that performs reliably in diverse environments, from busy urban developments to remote infrastructure projects. Burgenton Self-Loading Mobile Concrete Mixers are designed to meet these challenges, delivering dependable performance, durability, and mobility where they are needed most.
