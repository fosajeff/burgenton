our services

Our expertise covers

Geotechnical Engineering

Comprehensive analysis and design of earth-structure interactions to ensure safety and stability.

Offshore Engineering and Facilities

Engineering solutions for offshore platforms, foundations, pipelines, and coastal structures.

Soil Mechanics

Investigation of soil properties and behavior to inform foundation and earthwork designs.

Rock Mechanics

Evaluation of rock stability for tunnels, slopes, foundations, and mining projects.

Concrete Testing

In-situ and laboratory testing of concrete for quality assurance and compliance.

Site Investigation and Characterization

Detailed subsurface investigations using drilling, sampling, and in-situ testing.

Slope Stability Analysis

Evaluation and mitigation of slope failure risks for natural and man-made slopes.

Earthworks Design and Monitoring

Planning and monitoring of excavation, filling, and compaction activities.

Pile Load Testing and Design

Static and dynamic testing of piles for optimal design and performance assurance.

Ground Improvement Techniques

Implementation of soil stabilization methods such as grouting, compaction, and reinforcement.

Environmental Site Assessments

Analysis of soil and groundwater contamination and remediation planning.

Geophysical Surveys

Non-invasive subsurface mapping using electrical resistivity, seismic, and GPR methods.

Seepage and Drainage Studies

Modeling and design of groundwater and surface water flow control systems.

Pavement Design and Evaluation

Engineering of road and runway pavement systems for durability and cost-efficiency.

Retaining Structure Design

Design of gravity, cantilever, and anchored retaining walls to resist earth pressures.

Settlement Analysis and Monitoring

Prediction and observation of ground movements for structures and embankments.

Instrumentation and Monitoring Services

Installation and monitoring of geotechnical instruments for project safety and performance.

Borehole Drilling and Logging

Execution and documentation of subsurface boreholes for soil and rock profiling.

Hydrogeological Studies

Analysis of groundwater flow, aquifer properties, and water table behavior.
